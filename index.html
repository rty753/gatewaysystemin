Common Questions

How does your Payment Gateway Management System work?

Our system provides a complete solution for gaming businesses to process payments, manage transactions, and optimize conversion rates. It includes payment processing, monitoring tools, fraud prevention, and reporting capabilities all in one platform.

Can I rent the entire Payment Gateway system?

Yes! We offer flexible rental options for our entire Payment Gateway Management System. This allows gaming businesses to access enterprise-level payment infrastructure without significant upfront investment. Contact us for rental pricing options.

What gaming payment methods do you support?

Our gateway supports all popular Indian payment methods including UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards, net banking, and e-wallets. We also support international payment options for global players accessing Indian gaming platforms.

How do your OTP alerts and monitoring bots work?

Our intelligent system monitors OTP delivery and authentication in real-time. If issues are detected, our bots automatically implement alternative verification methods to complete transactions. The system also includes proactive monitoring for disbursement security to prevent account freezes.
